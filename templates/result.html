How to understand word importance:

Word importance describes how much that word contributes to the sentiment of the tweet according to our model. The sign indicates its sentiment(>0 for positive), and the magnitude indicates the strength of that sentiment. When a positive word is removed, the confidence of the prediction change in the negative direction drops by the word's importance(i.e. if the sentiment is negative, it becomes more negative, and vice versa).
